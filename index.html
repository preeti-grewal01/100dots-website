HELLOSCHOOL ERP

Everything a School Needs

20+ modules covering every aspect of school management. One system. Three platforms.

👨‍🎓
Student Management
Complete student profiles, enrollment, promotion, TC generation, and academic year tracking.
📋
Assignments & Homework
Create, assign, and grade assignments. Students submit online. File attachments supported.
✅
Attendance Tracking
Daily attendance with Present/Absent/Late. Weekly and monthly reports with percentage analytics.
💰
Fee Management
Fee structures, installment tracking, payment recording, and auto-generated PDF receipts.
📢
Circulars & Announcements
Send class-specific circulars with file attachments. Mark urgent notices. Track views.
📊
Report Cards
4-stage workflow: Draft → Submitted → Authorized → Published. PDF download for parents.

Staff Management
Staff profiles, attendance, leave tracking, and role-based access control.
💬
Communication
Bidirectional teacher-student messaging with inbox, sent, unread badges, and reply threading.
⏰
Timetables
Upload and manage class timetables. Latest badge highlights newest timetable. Download support.
🖼️
School Gallery
Upload and organize school event photos. Visible to all users on web and mobile.
📈
Analytics & Reports
Attendance reports, fee summaries, submission tracking, and system health monitoring.
📱
Mobile App (Android)
Full-featured Flutter mobile app for teachers, students, and admins. Works on all Android phones.
